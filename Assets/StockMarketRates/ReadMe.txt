Stock Market Rates - ReadMe

Ever wanted to show real stock market rates & indicators in your game or app? - Look no further! The Stock Market Rates components gives you easy access to the stock market, including rates and charts.
The Stock Market Rates package contains two key components:
1) Stock Rate Charts - This component will allow you to render Stock Market Rates to a charted image. There are over 8 different periods to choose from for any NSE Symbol.
2) Stock Rate Quotes - This component allows you to retrieve the latest market rates/quotes to use in your game.

To add either of the components to your scene simply attach them to a game object or canvas and set the Symbol to the stock that you want to monitor.
